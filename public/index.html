Short URLs

This is the domain for my short URLs. You may find links similar to byjp.fyi/me, they come here and will redirect you to the (likely more complicated) URL I intended.

I run this domain myself so that the redirect destinations are public, and will remain accessible, even as links rot. It's powered by IPFS _redirects, you can see the full list of redirects at byjp.fyi/_redirects any time.
